# VCT Platform Legal & Compliance Architecture

As the official digital platform for the Vietnamese Traditional Martial Arts Federation, strict adherence to legal, data privacy, and financial compliance is non-negotiable. This document outlines the absolute rules that all developers and AI agents must follow to protect the platform from legal liability.

## 1. Terms of Service & Electronic Consent
- **Versioned Agreements**: All "Terms of Service" (Điều khoản sử dụng), "Privacy Policies" (Chính sách bảo mật), and "Competition Rules" (Điều lệ thi đấu) must be stored with explicit version numbers (e.g., `v1.2.0`).
- **Cryptographic Consent Logging**: When a user registers or accepts a policy, the system must generate an immutable audit log row containing: `<User_UUID>`, `<Agreement_Type>`, `<Agreement_Version>`, `<Timestamp_UTC>`, `<Client_IP>`, and `<User_Agent>`.
- **Forced Re-consent**: If the Federation updates a policy to a breaking version, the authentication/routing middleware (`middleware.ts` or Go Auth Check) MUST intercept the user and force explicit re-consent before allowing them to access the application.

## 2. Minor Protection & Parental Guardianship (COPPA Equivalent)
- **Strict Age Gating**: Any Athlete entity with a `DateOfBirth` making them strictly under 18 years old MUST have a verified `Parent/Guardian` relation.
- **Delegated Authority (Waivers)**: Minors cannot legally sign injury waivers or consent forms for tournaments. All tournament registrations, belt exams, and high-risk activities for minors MUST trigger an approval workflow requiring explicit digital consent from the linked Parent/Guardian account.

## 3. Data Privacy & The Right to be Forgotten
- **Absolutely No Hard Deletes**: Running `DELETE FROM athletes WHERE ...` is universally BANNED. Deletion must strictly be implemented as a "Soft Delete" by setting a `deleted_at` timestamp. 
- **Anonymization over Deletion**: If an athlete exercises their legal "Right to be Forgotten", their Personally Identifiable Information (PII) like `full_name`, `email`, `phone_number`, and `address` must be overwritten with scrambled/anonymized values.
- **Historical Integrity**: Under no circumstances can an athlete's historical match records or belt progressions be deleted. The system must retain their generic `Athlete_UUID` to ensure previous opponents' ELO scores and historical tournament bracket structures remain perfectly intact.
- **Data Export**: The platform must provide an API endpoint returning a standard JSON/CSV representation of all data held on a user if they submit a "Data Subject Access Request".

## 4. Official Federation Documents & Immutable Storage
- **Stamped PDF Integrity**: Official documents (e.g., "Điều lệ giải PDF có dấu mộc") uploaded by organizing committees must be stored in Immutable Cloud Storage (S3/MinIO with Object Lock enabled). 
- **Hash Verification**: The system must track the SHA-256 hash of these files to prevent malicious/silent replacement of tournament rules mid-event.
- **Digital Certificates**: Any certificate (Chứng nhận đai, Bằng khen) generated by the system must include a QR code linking securely to the VCT Platform verification endpoint (e.g., `https://vct.vn/verify/{uuid}`) to prove authenticity against forgery.

## 5. Financial & Tax Accounting Compliance
- **Immutable Invoices**: A generated and paid `Invoice` record is a sacred financial document. Modifying the amount, line items, or tax data of a finalized invoice is BANNED.
- **Credit Notes (Hóa đơn điều chỉnh)**: If an invoice was issued incorrectly, the system must issue a subsequent "Credit Note" offsetting the original invoice, creating a clear GAAP-compliant audit trail.
- **Webhook Securirty**: All financial webhook callbacks (from VNPay, MoMo, Stripe) MUST have their HMAC/cryptographic signatures strictly verified before modifying any database state.
